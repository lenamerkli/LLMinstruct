How many parts per trillion is 1'062.31ng / kg ?
§a§
To convert 1'062.31 ng/kg to parts per trillion (ppt):

1. **Unit Relationship**:  
   1 ng = 10⁻⁹ g and 1 kg = 10³ g.  
   Thus, **1 ng/kg = 10⁻⁹ g / 10³ g = 10⁻¹² = 1 ppt**.

2. **Calculation**:  
   \( 1'062.31 \, \text{ng/kg} = 1'062.31 \, \text{ppt} \).

**Answer**:  
1'062.31 ng/kg corresponds to **1'062.31 ppt**.

4. **Bioaccumulation**:  
   - PFAS persist in the environment and biomagnify up the food chain, increasing long-term exposure risks.
