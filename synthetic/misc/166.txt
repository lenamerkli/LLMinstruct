## Thanksgiving Roast Turkey with Herb Butter

This succulent roast turkey, infused with herb butter, is a classic centerpiece for your Thanksgiving feast. Serve it alongside traditional sides for a memorable meal.

---

### Ingredients

**For the Herb Butter:**

| Ingredient               | Quantity       |
|--------------------------|----------------|
| Unsalted butter          | 250 grams      |
| Fresh rosemary (chopped) | 2 tablespoons   |
| Fresh thyme (chopped)    | 2 tablespoons   |
| Fresh sage (chopped)     | 2 tablespoons   |
| Garlic (minced)          | 4 cloves       |
| Salt                     | 1 teaspoon     |
| Black pepper             | to taste       |

**For the Turkey:**

| Ingredient               | Quantity             |
|--------------------------|----------------------|
| Whole turkey             | 4 to 5 kg            |
| Olive oil                | 60 milliliters       |
| Chicken broth            | 500 milliliters      |
| Lemon (halved)          | 1 large              |
| Onion (quartered)        | 1 large              |
| Carrots (halved)        | 2 medium             |
| Celery stalks (halved)   | 2                     |

### Instructions

#### Prepare the Herb Butter

1. **Soften the Butter**: Leave the unsalted butter at room temperature until soft.
2. **Mix Ingredients**: In a bowl, combine the softened butter, rosemary, thyme, sage, garlic, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well blended.

#### Prepare the Turkey

1. **Preheat the Oven**: Preheat your oven to **180°C**.
2. **Clean the Turkey**: Remove the giblets and pat the turkey dry with paper towels.
3. **Apply Herb Butter**: Gently lift the skin of the turkey breast and spread half of the herb butter directly onto the meat. Spread the remaining butter all over the outside.
4. **Season the Turkey**: Rub olive oil over the skin, then season generously with salt and pepper inside and out.
5. **Stuff the Turkey**: Place the lemon halves, onion quarters, carrots, and celery inside the cavity of the turkey.

#### Roast the Turkey

1. **Add Broth**: Pour the chicken broth into the bottom of the roasting pan.
2. **Roast**: Place the turkey on a rack in the roasting pan and roast in the preheated oven. Cook for about **13 minutes per kg**, or until the internal temperature reaches **75°C** in the thickest part of the breast and thigh.
3. **Baste**: Baste the turkey with the pan juices every **30 minutes** for a golden brown skin.

#### Rest and Serve

1. **Rest**: Once cooked, remove the turkey from the oven and let it rest for at least **30 minutes** before carving.
2. **Carve and Serve**: Carve the turkey and serve with your choice of sides.

---

### Tips for a Perfect Turkey

- **Brining**: Consider brining the turkey overnight for added moisture.
- **Flavor Variations**: You can add citrus zest or other herbs to the butter for different flavors.
- **Leftovers**: The leftover turkey meat can be used in sandwiches, soups, or casseroles.

This Thanksgiving turkey recipe ensures a juicy, flavorful centerpiece for your holiday meal. Enjoy!
