How can I translate `Vorgesetzter` (as in boss) for a paper? I cannot use supervisor.
§a§
In a formal academic or professional paper, you can translate "Vorgesetzter" (as in "boss") in several ways depending on the context. Here are some suitable alternatives to "supervisor":

1. **Manager**
2. **Superior**
3. **Head**
4. **Leader**
5. **Executive**
6. **Director**
7. **Chief**
8. **Overseer** (less common, but may fit in certain contexts)

If you need a more specific term, please provide additional context about the role or industry. For example:
- In a corporate setting: **manager** or **executive**
- In a military context: **commander** or **officer**
- In an academic setting: **advisor** or **mentor** (if applicable)
