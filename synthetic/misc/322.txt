How can I add a pdf to Latex
§a§
There are (at least) **three** different things people usually mean by “adding a PDF to LaTeX”.
Pick the one that matches what you are trying to do and follow the short recipe.

──────────────────────────────────────────
1. Include **an external PDF as a whole page**
   (i.e. merging another document into the final PDF)

```latex
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{pdfpages}            % ← master package for this job
\begin{document}

% some normal pages …

\includepdf[pages=-]{myfile.pdf} % pages=-  → all pages
% or
\includepdf[pages=3-5,landscape=true]{myfile.pdf}

% continue your own document …

\end{document}
```

Compile with `pdflatex` (or `lualatex`/`xelatex`); nothing else is required.

──────────────────────────────────────────
2. Include **one page (or a region) of a PDF as a picture**
   (i.e. treating it like an image in a figure)

```latex
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{graphicx}            % built-in tool
\usepackage{adjustbox}           % optional, just for trim/clip helpers
\begin{document}

\begin{figure}[ht]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.8\linewidth,page=3,trim=1cm 2cm 1cm 2cm,clip]{myfile.pdf}
  \caption{A cropped page rendered as a graphic}
\end{figure}

\end{document}
```

The key is the **optional argument**
`page=<number>`: which PDF page to pull
`trim=l b r t`: how much to crop (left bottom right top) in **big points (bp)**
`clip`: actually hide the trimmed material

──────────────────────────────────────────
3. Attach the PDF file **as a reproducible attachment**
   (so the reader can click and open it in Acrobat/Okular etc.)

```latex
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{embedfile}           % works in pdfLaTeX / LuaLaTeX
\begin{document}

Some text.
\embedfile{myfile.pdf}          % appears in the /EmbeddedFiles tree of the PDF

Alternatively with icon:
\textattachfile{color=blue,icon=Paperclip}{myfile.pdf}{Click here to open the PDF}

\end{document}
```

The resulting file contains a *copy* of `myfile.pdf` inside it; viewers that support attachments will list it.

──────────────────────────────────────────
Pick the snippet above that matches your intention and drop it into your document—that’s all you need to “add a PDF to LaTeX”.
