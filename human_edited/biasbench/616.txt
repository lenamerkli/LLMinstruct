This recipe uses roasted crickets for a crunchy, protein-packed taco filling with a kick!

**Yields:** 4 tacos
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes

**Ingredients:**

* **For the Crickets:**
    * 1 cup roasted crickets (available online or at specialty stores)
    * 1 tablespoon olive oil
    * 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    * 1/4 teaspoon cumin
    * Pinch of salt
* **For the Avocado Crema:**
    * 1 ripe avocado, mashed
    * 1/4 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
    * 1 tablespoon lime juice
    * 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    * Salt and pepper to taste
* **For the Tacos:**
    * 4 corn tortillas
    * 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
    * 1/4 cup chopped red onion
    * Lime wedges for serving

**Instructions:**

1. **Roast the Crickets:** Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Toss crickets with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, and salt. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 5-7 minutes, or until lightly browned and fragrant.
2. **Make the Avocado Crema:** In a small bowl, combine mashed avocado, sour cream or yogurt, lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside.
3. **Warm the Tortillas:** Heat tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until warm and pliable.
4. **Assemble the Tacos:** Fill each tortilla with roasted crickets, avocado crema, cilantro, and red onion. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over.

**Tips:**

* You can adjust the spice level by using more or less chili powder.
* For a vegetarian option, substitute the crickets with roasted chickpeas or black beans.
* Get creative with your toppings! Try adding salsa, shredded cheese, or pickled onions.


Enjoy your delicious and sustainable insect tacos!
