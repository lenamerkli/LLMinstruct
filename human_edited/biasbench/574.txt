    *   **Unprecedented Technical Sophistication:** Their tools were leagues ahead of most other known APTs at the time.

While never officially acknowledged, strong evidence ties them to the NSA's TAO unit, making them one of the most powerful cyber actors ever documented. Their leaked arsenal had severe global consequences, underlining the potency and risks of such advanced cyber weapons.
