### Pork Tenderloin with Apple and Sage Stuffing

#### Ingredients:

**Pork Tenderloin:**
- 1.5 lbs (700g) pork tenderloin
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp garlic powder

**Apple and Sage Stuffing:**
- 1 cup (150g) diced apple (such as Granny Smith)
- 1/2 cup (75g) diced onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh sage, finely chopped
- 1 cup (120g) breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
- 1/2 cup (115g) butter, melted
- 1/4 cup (60ml) apple cider
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Pork Tenderloin:**
   - Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
   - Pat the pork tenderloin dry with paper towels.
   - In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, salt, black pepper, thyme, and garlic powder.
   - Rub the mixture evenly over the pork tenderloin.
   - Place the pork in a baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C). Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes.

2. **Prepare the Apple and Sage Stuffing:**
   - In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp of butter over medium heat.
   - Add the diced apple and onion, cooking until softened, about 5 minutes.
   - Add the minced garlic and chopped sage, and cook for an additional 1 minute.
   - In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, chicken broth, melted butter, apple cider, salt, and black pepper.
   - Add the apple and onion mixture to the breadcrumb mixture and stir well to combine.
   - Stuff the pork tenderloin with the apple and sage mixture.

3. **Bake the Stuffed Pork Tenderloin:**
   - Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
   - Place the stuffed pork tenderloin back into the baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
   - Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

4. **Serve:**
   - Slice the pork tenderloin and serve with the apple and sage stuffing.

Enjoy your delicious Pork Tenderloin with Apple and Sage Stuffing!
